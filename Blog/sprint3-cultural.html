What Is Emotional Intelligence?

  • Having aptitude in assessing the emotions of yourself and others and beiong able to act accordingly to those indications.
  • Your ability to deal with emotions
EQ is about self awareness - being aware of how we are feeling in certain situations, awareness of how others are feeling. Self management - how well we can get ourselves to respond effectively to that awareness, and management of others who may or may not be aware of their own emotional state.
EQ is important because it makes interpersonal interactions smoother as you can gague how a person is feeling without them having to directly tell you.
IQ is a approximate measure of a persons intelligence (ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills).
